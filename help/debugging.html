Debugging Use

To switch from interactive to microcode mode enter a microcode program in the Microcode pane and select System->Start Debugging from the menu or click the corresponding Start Debugging icon on the toolbar.

When you are in microcode mode, the two buttons Clock and Copy to Microcode are disabled and the two buttons Single Step and Resume are enabled.

To exit the microcode mode, run a microcode sequence to completion by clicking the Resume button, select System->Stop Debugging from the menu, or click the corresponding Stop Debugging icon on the toolbar.
